**Summary**: Expanded from 10 to 12 dimensions by adding Craft and Testing at 1.5x weight. Honest score dropped from inflated 8.0 to 6.6.

## Decision
Added Craft (#11) and Testing (#12) because a co-creator that doesn't follow specs and doesn't test is just a fast typist. Both weighted 1.5x to reflect their importance.

## Rationale
Previous 10-dimension score was 8.0 but missed the two things that matter most for a builder: does it build what was specified (Craft) and does it prove its work is correct (Testing).

## Impact
Score dropped from 8.0 to 6.6. This is correct — it surfaces real gaps instead of hiding them.
