> Appended by session-save.sh Stop hook. Audited weekly by `/evolve audit`.

## Signal Key

- вң… `accept` вҖ” Gary used output without changes
- рҹ”„ `rework` вҖ” Gary asked for changes
- вқҢ `reject` вҖ” Gary discarded output
- рҹҺҜ `love` вҖ” Gary explicitly praised output
- рҹ”Ғ `repeat` вҖ” Gary had to re-explain something
- вҸӯпёҸ `skip` вҖ” Gary skipped a prescribed step
