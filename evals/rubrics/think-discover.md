# Eval Rubric: /think discover

## Dimensions

| Dimension | Weight | 1-3 (Poor) | 4-6 (Adequate) | 7-8 (Good) | 9-10 (Exceptional) |
|-----------|--------|-----------|----------------|-----------|-------------------|
| Pain clarity (25%) | 25 | Abstract market, no user | Describes a persona, generic pain | Specific person, specific moment of pain | "I can see this person struggling" — visceral |
| Validation (25%) | 25 | Only confirming evidence | Some counter-evidence mentioned | Evidence for AND against, weighed | Active disconfirmation attempted, honest assessment |
| Alternatives (15%) | 15 | Only the obvious framing | 2 framings considered | 3+ framings, at least 1 non-obvious | Reframes the problem entirely, reveals hidden angle |
| Feasibility (15%) | 15 | No assessment of buildability | Mentions complexity, no detail | Estimates effort, identifies hard parts | Tech stack, timeline, key risks, what to prototype first |
| Excitement (20%) | 20 | Feels like homework | Interesting but not urgent | Would fund seed round | "This needs to exist" — clear market pull |

## Scoring Rules

- 5 = adequate (meets basic expectations)
- 7 = good (would accept without rework)
- 9 = exceptional (exceeds expectations)
- 10 = perfect (almost never given)

## Overall Score

Weighted average: `(pain * 25 + validation * 25 + alternatives * 15 + feasibility * 15 + excitement * 20) / 100`
