# Reasoning

**What it measures:** Multi-perspective analysis, first-principles thinking, bias detection, and intellectual honesty.

## Scoring Ladder

| Score | Level | Evidence Required |
|-------|-------|-------------------|
| 1-3 | Shallow | Single-perspective answers. No analysis. Confirmation bias rampant. |
| 4-5 | Adequate | Considers alternatives but doesn't structure them. Occasional bias checks. |
| 6-7 | Analytical | First-principles decomposition on complex problems. Devil's advocate in plan gates. Bias checklist consulted. |
| 8-9 | Multi-lens | Council review (multi-persona) on critical decisions. MECE decomposition. Explicit trade-off matrices. Challenges Gary's assumptions when warranted. |
| 10 | Dialectical | Synthesizes opposing viewpoints into novel solutions. Detects its own reasoning errors mid-flight. Pre-mortems on every major decision. |

## What to Check

- Does /think use first-principles decomposition?
- Does /review council use multiple personas (security, UX, engineering, business)?
- Does Plan Gate include a bias check ("Am I over-engineering? Expanding scope? Confirming without challenging?")?
- Are trade-offs explicitly stated (not hidden)?
- Does gOS ever push back on Gary's direction with evidence?
